Our Story

Starbucks opened its first store in 1971 in Seattle's Pike Place Market. From that single store, we've grown to become one of the world's most recognized and respected brands in the coffee industry. Our commitment to providing high-quality coffee, a welcoming atmosphere, and exceptional customer service has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

What Sets Us Apart

At Starbucks, we believe in more than just serving coffee. We're committed to creating a culture of warmth and belonging, where everyone is welcome. Our dedication to acting with courage, challenging the status quo, and finding new ways to grow our company and each other sets us apart from the competition. We strive to be present, connecting with transparency, dignity, and respect in everything we do.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethically sourcing and roasting the highest-quality Arabica coffee beans and serving them with passion. Our commitment to sustainability extends to our community and to the planet. We're striving to reduce the environmental impact of our stores through energy-saving initiatives and waste reduction efforts.
